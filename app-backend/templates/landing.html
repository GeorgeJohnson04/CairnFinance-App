Security that protects your data

  • Your eyes only. Every record is bound to your account. Other users can never see your data.
  • Argon2id hashing. A modern, memory-hard password algorithm. Your password is never stored anywhere.
  • Hardened sessions. HttpOnly, SameSite cookies backed by revocable server-side sessions, so you can sign out everywhere at any time.
  • CSRF and strict CSP. Every form is token-protected and a strict Content-Security-Policy blocks injected scripts.
  • Rate-limited sign-in. Brute-force attempts are throttled automatically.
